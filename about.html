Our Story

Founded in 1995, PrimeEstate began with a simple mission: to help
families and businesses find their perfect properties. What started as a
small office in New York has grown into one of the most trusted real
estate agencies in the nation.

Over the past 28 years, we've helped thousands of clients realize their
dreams of homeownership and smart property investments. Our success
is built on unwavering commitment to integrity, professionalism, and
personalized service.

Today, PrimeEstate operates in 25+ cities nationwide, with a team of 50+
expert agents who share our passion for excellence. We combine decades
of market expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver unparalleled
results for our clients.
